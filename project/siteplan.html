Site Plan for Beulahland Christian School

Site Name

Beulahland Christian School

This name represents a vibrant and welcoming school that aims to provide quality education and foster a nurturing environment for students. The site will be the main source of information for students, parents, and staff.

Optional domain availability: @bcsghana.org

Site Purpose

The site serves as a central hub for all school-related information, including announcements, event calendars, staff directories, student resources, and parent information. It aims to enhance communication within the school community.

Scenarios

  • How can I access the school calendar for upcoming events?
  • Where can I find contact information for my child's teacher?

Color Schema

  • Brick Red (#CB4154): Used for headings, navigation bars, and accents to represent trust and authority.
  • Light Yellow (#FFF9C4): Used for background and sections to give a cheerful and bright feel.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Lato', sans-serif - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for body text and paragraphs.
